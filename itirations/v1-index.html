Who We Are

A legacy built on trust and precision

26 Years of Excellence

Founded in 1998 by structural engineer Mr Ajith Kumar, Charunya Associates began as a modest residential firm in Bengaluru. Through meticulous craftsmanship and an unwavering commitment to quality, we grew into one of South India's most respected construction companies.

Today, our portfolio spans premium residences, corporate campuses, institutional buildings, and large-scale infrastructure — each project a testament to our core belief that construction is both a science and an art.

With over 180 completed projects and a team of 320 skilled professionals, we continue to raise the standard of what's possible in the built environment.
